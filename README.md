# Metrics-of-Issued-Bonds-Term-Deposit-Agreements
Shows calculated metrics of Issued Bonds and Term Deposit agreements for the last two years.


# Business Objectives
Analyze the Bonds & Term Deposits, (Number of agreements and volume).
Observe signed and at the same time closed Bond and Term deposit agrements within the same month, (Number of agreements and volume).
Analyze the weighted average duration, weighted average interest rate of the signed Bonds and Term Deposit agreements.
Analyze the digital share of the signed Bond and Term Deposit agreements, (Number of agreements and volume).


# Data Model
Dim Tables: Funds_involved_in_Bonds_Dim_By_Date,Deposit_Dim_By_Date, Clients_Dim_By_Date
Fact Tables: CB_Exchange_Rate
